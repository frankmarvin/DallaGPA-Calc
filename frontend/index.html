Frequently Asked Questions

How is GPA calculated?

GPA = (sum of (credits × grade point)) ÷ total credits. Our system does it automatically.

Can I customise the grading system?

Yes, administrators can create and assign grading systems per university.

Is the system secure?

Absolutely – JWT tokens, HTTPS, and role‑based access control protect your data.
